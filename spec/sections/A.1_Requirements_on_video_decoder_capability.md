**A.1** **Requirements on video decoder capability**


Capabilities of video decoders conforming to this Recommendation | International Standard are specified in terms of the
ability to decode video streams conforming to the constraints of profiles and levels specified in this annex. For each such
profile, the level supported for that profile shall also be expressed.


Specific values are specified in this annex for the syntax elements profile_idc and level_idc. All other values of profile_idc
and level_idc are reserved for future use by ITU-T | ISO/IEC.
